Frequently asked questions

Why use short videos for updates? add
Because nobody reads release notes. Vertical videos grab attention, explain what's new in seconds, and feel intuitive — just like the content users already engage with on social platforms.
Where do these videos appear? add
Right inside your app or product interface. Users see them in context — when and where it matters most — without needing to visit external docs or wait for emails.
How is this better than tooltips or banners? add
Tooltips show where something changed. Video updates show why it matters — with visuals, motion, and voice. It's a richer, more human way to communicate change.
Can I personalize updates for different users? add
Yes. You can segment by role, plan, behavior, or product usage — so every user sees updates that are actually relevant to them.
Can I schedule videos in advance? add
Absolutely. You can publish on a schedule, set expiration dates, and plan ahead for phased rollouts or launches.
How do I track performance? add
Coolstory gives you analytics on views, completion rates, and click-throughs — so you can see what resonates and what's getting ignored.
Can I run promotions or discounts in my Stories? add
No. It works for mobile apps, internal tools, service platforms, and any digital product that evolves regularly and needs a better way to keep users informed.
What if I don't have video content yet? add
No problem. You can start with simple screen recordings, voiceovers, or even use Coolstory's AI tools to turn written updates into videos. You don't need a full video team to make it work.
