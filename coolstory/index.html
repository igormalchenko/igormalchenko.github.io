Privacy and security

web

Minimal page access

The widget queries for known selectors inside its container instead of scanning the whole DOM or walking global state. It doesn't intercept submissions or read input values.

search

Transparent calls

All requests from the widget go to a small, predictable set of endpoints over HTTPS, so it's easy to allowlist and monitor in egress and CSP rules.

lock

Request-only data

The widget records only the interaction signals you configure (e.g., view, click, completion) and includes just the metadata needed to attribute the event.

Frequently asked questions

How does Coolstory work? add
Coolstory helps you communicate with your userbase by providing stories as a service in a single product. As soon as it's integrated into your app, you can create and post stories for your users to see straight from the Coolstory web or mobile apps.
How do I post a story? add
It's easily done by going to a Coolstory app on either web or mobile and uploading the video just like you would do on Instagram or TikTok. Once the video is uploaded, you post it to your userbase or some of the segments.
Do I need engineers to make it work? add
Yes, you need someone with engineering background to integrate the SDK into your app. We've made it really simple, so it should take less than an hour of work.
How does it help with engagement? add
All social platforms are adopting short-form content for a reason — that's the current way of absorbing information. Coolstory is bringing this familiar format to your B2B setting, where customers will learn your product via short-form content instead of massive knowledge bases.
Do you offer a trial? add
Yes, all our plans come with a 14-day unlimited trial to make sure you can set up and use your new stories functionality.
