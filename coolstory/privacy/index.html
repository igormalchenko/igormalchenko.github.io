1. Introduction

1.1. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you access or use the Coolstory Service ("Service").

2.1. Personal Information: We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for an account, subscribe to a plan, contact us, or otherwise interact with the Service. This information may include your name, email address, and any other details you choose to provide.

2.2. Usage Data: We automatically collect information on how you access and use the Service, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and the dates and times of your visits.

2.3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather information about usage trends. You may adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies; however, this may affect your ability to use some parts of the Service.

3.1. We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Monitor and analyze usage trends and preferences;
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security breaches.

3.2. We may also use your information for marketing purposes, subject to your preferences, and to comply with legal obligations.

4. Disclosure of Information

4.1. Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Service, subject to confidentiality obligations.

4.2. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, or in response to a valid request by public authorities.

4.3. Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable privacy safeguards.

5. Data Security

5.1. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6.1. We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

6.2. When the retention period expires, your information will be securely deleted or anonymized.
