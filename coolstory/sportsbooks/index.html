Frequently asked questions

Why use stories in a sportsbook? add
Because banners, modals, and tooltips don't cut it anymore. Stories give you a flexible, native way to guide users, promote content, and increase engagement—without cluttering the interface.
Can I use stories to boost signups or deposits? add
Yes. Many customers use homepage stories to show off platform features before signup, and onboarding stories to guide users through deposit steps and bonus activation.
Do I need developers to run the campaigns? add
Only once—for integration. After that, product, CRM, or marketing teams can create, schedule, and publish stories on their own.
Will this work on both sportsbook and casino products? add
Definitely. Many customers run parallel campaigns for sportsbook and casino, using stories to cross-promote and guide users across product lines.
Can I schedule videos in advance? add
Absolutely. You can publish on a schedule, set expiration dates, and plan ahead for phased rollouts or launches.
How do I track performance? add
Coolstory gives you analytics on views, completion rates, and click-throughs — so you can see what resonates and what's getting ignored.
Can I run promotions or discounts in my Stories? add
No. It works for mobile apps, internal tools, service platforms, and any digital product that evolves regularly and needs a better way to keep users informed.
What if I don't have video content yet? add
No problem. You can start with simple screen recordings, voiceovers, or even use Coolstory's AI tools to turn written updates into videos. You don't need a full video team to make it work.
