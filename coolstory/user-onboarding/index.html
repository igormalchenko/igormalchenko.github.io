Frequently asked questions

How is this different from a product tour? add
Product tours are step-by-step overlays that block your UI and feel intrusive. Coolstory stories are a native format users already love — they watch them voluntarily, on their own time, inside the app. Completion rates are dramatically higher.
Can I target stories to new users only? add
Yes. Coolstory's segmentation lets you target stories by any user attribute — including account age, onboarding step, plan type, or any custom property you pass. New users see onboarding stories; power users don't.
How long does it take to set up? add
Integration takes less than an hour of engineering time. After that, anyone on your team can create and publish onboarding stories — no code required. Most customers have their first story live within the same day.
What formats work for onboarding stories? add
Short videos work best — screen recordings of your product are perfect. You can also use images and GIFs. Stories support CTAs, buttons, and deep links so users can take action directly from the story.
Can I track who completed onboarding? add
Yes. Coolstory tracks views, completion rates, and CTA clicks for every story. You can see which users watched your onboarding content and which dropped off, so you know exactly where to improve.
Do you offer a trial? add
Yes, all plans come with a 14-day unlimited trial so you can set up your onboarding stories and see results before committing.
