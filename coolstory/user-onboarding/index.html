Frequently asked questions

Isn't video overkill for onboarding? add
Not when it's short and focused. You're not making a training course — you're showing users what matters, quickly and clearly. One 20-second video can do what 5 tooltips can't.
We already have tooltips — isn't that enough? add
Tooltips show users where to click. Video shows them why it matters. Most users don't learn by hovering — they learn by seeing things in action.
Will this slow down the experience? add
Not at all. It's designed to speed things up — show users what they need to know in seconds, not paragraphs.
Why not just link to docs or help center articles? add
Because users don't read them. If something isn't right in front of them, it's ignored. Video onboarding meets users where they are — in the product, in the moment.
How customizable is the experience? add
Fully. You can control the video order, appearance, branding, and which user segments see what — so onboarding fits your product, not the other way around.
Can I combine this with existing onboarding flows? add
Yes. You can use video alongside checklists, modals, or product tours — or replace them entirely for a cleaner experience.
Does it work in different languages? add
Yep. You can upload localized content or create language-specific flows for international users, ecommerce platforms, and any digital product that evolves regularly and needs a better way to keep users informed.
Can videos trigger based on user actions? add
They can. You can trigger videos contextually — on first login, after a key event, or when a user reaches a specific milestone.
