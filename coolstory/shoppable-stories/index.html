Frequently asked questions

What exactly are Shoppable Stories? add
Shoppable Stories are interactive, visual experiences where users can explore and purchase products directly from the content. With Coolstory, you can create these stories and seamlessly integrate them into your website or marketing campaigns.
How do Shoppable Stories work? add
In a Shoppable Story, brands showcase products within images, videos, or other content formats. Each product is tagged with a link, so users can click to purchase directly from the story without leaving the page.
Why should my business use Shoppable Stories? add
Shoppable Stories provide a more engaging way to showcase products and drive conversions. They allow businesses to reach customers in a format that's interactive and visually appealing, which can improve the shopping experience and increase sales.
Are Shoppable Stories only for social media? add
Yes! With Coolstory, you can embed Shoppable Stories directly on your website, blog, or e-commerce store — giving you full ownership over your content and customer interactions.
How can I track the performance of my Shoppable Stories? add
Coolstory provides built-in analytics that shows you views, clicks, and purchases. This helps you understand engagement and optimize future stories for better results.
Can I use Coolstory for any type of product? add
Yes! Whether you're selling fashion, beauty, electronics, or home goods, Coolstory lets you create Shoppable Stories that fit your brand and product lineup.
Can I run promotions or discounts in my Stories? add
Yes! With Coolstory, you can highlight sales, promo codes, or special offers directly within your stories to drive conversions.
How do I start? add
1. Sign up to Coolstory. 2. Upload your content (photos, videos, text). 3. Tag your products and link them to your store. 4. Publish the story and embed it on your site.
